The Opportunity

Static menus leak revenue.

Existing kiosk systems suggest static items (like generic fries) to every customer. They fail to adapt to live contexts, wasting prime on-screen estate.

1

No Context Awareness

Standard recommendations ignore the active cart, time of day, and current discount promotions.

2

Dry and Impersonal Copy

Simple text like "You might also like: Pepsi" does not capture interest or motivate addition.

3

Unproven Value

Restaurants cannot measure potential ROI prior to expensive live field tests.

Technical Design

The Pipes & Filters Pipeline

Modular processing units linked by strict data contracts, allowing components to run independently offline or online.

A

Offline Mining: Scans historical transactions with Apriori to build an association rule-set.

B

Online Rerank: Intersects rule-set with cart, boosts by promotions & time of day.

C

GenAI copywriting: Shapes final persuasive proposal with local context.

The Algorithm

Business Pitch

Proving ROI: The Backtest

Judges love math, executives love profits. We built an order simulator that replays transaction logs through three models to calculate absolute value difference.

Our backtest replays 1,200 orders. Under static models, addition rates hover at 8%. Under our contextual, GenAI hybrid model, conversion rises to 23%, validating the AOV uplift.
